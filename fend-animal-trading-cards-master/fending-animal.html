Felis

Felis cat has a cute sight, hearing and smell, and their sense of touch is enhanced by long whiskers that protrude from their heads and bodies.

  • Scientific name: Felis catus
  • Lifespan: 2 – 16 years (In the wild)
  • Daily sleep: 58 – 67 days
  • Gestation period: 12 – 16 hours

felis is a domestic cat which is a small, typically furry, carnivorous mammal. It is often called house cats when kept as indoor pet or simply cat when there is no need to distinguish them from other felids and felines.
